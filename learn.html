Learn Chess

What is Chess?

Chess is a strategy game played between two players. The goal is to checkmate the opponent's king.

How the Pieces Move Watch the video to begin

  • Pawn: moves forward, captures diagonally
  • Rook: moves in straight lines
  • Knight: moves in an L-shape
  • Bishop: moves diagonally
  • Queen: moves in all directions
  • King: moves one square in any direction

Goal of the Game

Protect your king while trying to checkmate the opponent's king.
