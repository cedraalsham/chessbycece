Chess Tactics

What are Tactics?

Tactics are short-term moves that help you gain an immediate advantage in the game.

Common Tactics

Fork: One piece attacks two pieces at the same time.

Pin: A piece cannot move without exposing a more valuable piece behind it.

Skewer: Forcing a valuable piece to move and exposing another behind it.

Discovered Attack: Moving one piece to reveal an attack from another piece.

Tip

Always look for hanging pieces before making your move.
